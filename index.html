Phishing- The fraudulent action of sending e-mails to individuals while disguised as a reputable company to lure them in to revealing personal information. Flaming- The act of posting or sending offensive posts on the internet. Spamming- The use of e-mails to send unwanted bulk messages indiscriminately. All three of these, use e-mails or other messaging systems to send unwanted messages. Phishing is really the only one that is illegal, while the other two could possibly be illegal depending on how far the person takes the action.
